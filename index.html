Daniel Zeymer von Metnitz

Medical Student | Doctoral Researcher | Advocate | Lecturer in Biostatistics

Hello, my name is Daniel Zeymer von Metnitz. I am a last year Medical Student, passionate about medical image analysis, statistics and global health advocacy. As a doctoral researcher at Ueffing Lab (University of Tübingen) I focus on retinal image analysis. I am advocating as Vice Head of the ÖH SFU, clerk at the education department of the Austrian Students Union, and I am active within the Austrian Medical Students Union and International Federation of Medical Students Associations.

Enjoying sharing my skills with others, I am teaching assistant for several courses in biostatistics and also active as an IFMSA trainer. When I find the time, I also develop websites.
