## Web development
- **German Cardiac Arrest Registry** | [G-CAR](https://cardiacarrestregistry.de/) | 2022
  - G-CAR is the first national (German) registry including a long-term follow-up for adult OHCA patients with presumed underlying cardiovascular cause and long-term follow-up
  - development and design of website, currently updating and maintaining website and server for [Leipzig Heart Science gGmbH](https://leipzig-heart.de).
- **Austrian Medical Student Association** | <a href="https://amsa.at/">AMSA-Austria</a> | 2020
  - AMSA advocates for all medical students in Austria on a national and international level, since 1951 founding member of IFMSA
  - development and design of new Website in 2020, needs analysis, feedback rounds, project planning and timeline, sites: home, projects, become active, SCs, Exchange, LCs, International, Advocacy, about, Analytics, News, Events
- **Austrian Students Union at the Sigmund Freud University Vienna** | <a href="https://www.oeh-sfu.at/">ÖH SFU</a> | 2018
  - The ÖH SFU is the elected student body advocate for the interests of >5000 Students, act as the students’ political representation as well as offering services and hosting events
  - development of new Website and creation of new Corporate Identity, Sites including: Services, About, Contact, as well as SEO, Analytics with over 100 Visitors/day
- **Website of the Medical Student Association of ÖH SFU** | <a href="https://stvmed.at/">stvmed</a> | 2018
  - The stvmed is the elected student body advocate for the interests of >1100 medical Students, act as the students’ political representation as well as offering services and events
  - development of first Website and creation of new Corporate Identity
- **Fachschaft Medizin Tübingen** | <a href="https://fachschaftmedizin.de">FSmed</a> | 2016
  - FSMED is representing over >2500 students of the medical faculty of the University of Tübingen
  - development of Website and of the new Corporate Design, Project together with D. Hundertmark
