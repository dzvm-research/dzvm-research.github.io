My doctoral thesis is under supervision of [Prof. Marius Ueffing](https://scholar.google.de/citations?user=TjBcPPkAAAAJ&hl) and [J. Dietter](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=Dietter+J&cauthor_id=34707346).
