Hello, my name is Daniel Zeymer von Metnitz. I am a last year Medical Student, passionate about medical image analysis, statistics and global health advocacy. As a doctoral [researcher](/research/) at [Ueffing Lab](https://www.eye-tuebingen.de/ueffinglab/) (University of Tübingen) I focus on retinal image analysis. I am [advocating](/advocacy/) as Vice Head of the [ÖH SFU](https://oeh-sfu.at/), clerk at the education department of the [Austrian Students Union](https://oeh.ac.at/), and active within the [Austrian Medical Students Union](https://amsa.at/) and [International Federation of Medical Students Associations](https://ifmsa.org/).

Enjoying sharing my skills with others, I am [teaching](/teaching/) assistant for several courses in biostatistics and also active as an IFMSA [trainer](/advocacy/). When I find the time, I also develop [websites](/code/).
