Hello, my name is Daniel Zeymer von Metnitz. I am a last year Medical Student, passionate about medical image analysis, statistics and global health advocacy. I am part of [Ueffing Lab](https://www.eye-tuebingen.de/ueffinglab/) at the University of Tübingen where I am focusing my [research](/research/) on retina analysis.

My [research interests](/research/) focus on medical images analysis, retina image analysis .

Enjoying sharing my skills with others, I am [teaching](/teaching/) assistant for several courses in statistics. I am  also a an IFMSA Trainer and have been [advocating](/advocacy/) as Vice Head of the [ÖH SFU](https://oeh-sfu.at/), clerk at the education departement of the Austrian Students Union [ÖH](https://oeh.ac.at/) as well as at the Austrian Medical Students Union [AMSA](https://amsa.at/) for several years. I am also active within the International Federation of Medical Student Associations [IFMSA](https://ifmsa.org/). When I find the time, I also develop [websites](/code/).

See more in my [CV](/cv/).
